For a detailed walk-through of this process, including screenshots, see the Tarbell installation and set-up guide.

1

Log in to the Google Developer Console. Select "Create an empty project." Give your project a name, accept the terms and click "Create."

2

Enable Google Drive API in the "APIs & auth > APIs" section in the sidebar.

3

In "APIs & auth > Credentials", choose "Create New Client ID." Create credentials for an "installed app" and continue to the consent screen.

4

Enter a "Product Name" on the consent screen and hit "Save." Choose "Installed App" and "Other" from the pop-up that appears. Choose "Create Client ID."

5

Download your credentials by clicking "Download JSON."

6

Rename the file you just downloaded to client_secrets.json and save it somewhere. (By default, Tarbell will look for this file in your Downloads directory.)

7

Add your new credential file, and then authenticate it with Google.
